Usage Guidelines

Who can use these badges?
Any project built out of spite. You don't need to be listed on spiteprojects.com (but you should be).

What's the license?
CC0 / Public Domain. Use them however you want. We're not lawyers, and we don't care.

Can I modify them?
Sure, they're SVGs. Fork the repo and make your own. Or don't. We're not the boss of you.

Do I have to link back?
No, but it'd be nice. Help other spite-driven developers find their people.
